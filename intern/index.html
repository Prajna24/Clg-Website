Our Story

SANS was established with the aim of providing quality higher education on par with international standards. It persistently seeks and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education on a consistent basis. The global standards set at SANS in the field of teaching and research spur us on in our relentless pursuit of excellence. With steady steps, we continue our march forward. We look forward to meeting you here at SANS.
